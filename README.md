# naijahealth
up to date NAFDAC information for everyone...

starting from the [NAFDAC](https://nafdac.gov.ng) website, my idea is to have a simpler way for the nigerian people to have access to health information about 
- Drugs
- Food
- Chemicals
- Veterinary & Allied Products
- Vaccines, Biologicals & Medical Devices
- Cosmetics
- Narcotics & Controlled Substances

the reason is  because, it's quite hard having to look through all that information on [NAFDAC](https://nafdac.gov.ng).

thinking of building a RAG and serving it through whatsapp...
and maybe build apps for android / ios later.

we can also probably go to other sectors... who knows ? :)
